Message From Aby

Dear, Syfna

I’m happy to be here with you.Sometimes I think about how small conversations with you can mean so much to me—whether we’re joking, talking about random things, or just checking on each other.I also remember when we first met in the Roblox game. Before that, I almost never played, but now I often play together with you. Maybe by sharing your feelings, your burden will feel a little lighter. Don’t hesitate to tell me, okay? I’m always here.That’s all I want to say. My message for you is: take care of your prayers, take care of your health, stay happy, and keep being enthusiastic about learning.
